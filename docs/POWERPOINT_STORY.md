# PowerPoint Story

## Core framing

Robo-T-Rader is a productivity platform for teams building autonomous trading systems.

## Story arc

1. Trading automation is hard because research, operation, and governance are usually mixed together
2. Robo-T-Rader separates those layers into a coherent platform
3. Live environments operate
4. Hub standardizes data
5. Sim, VFL, Opportunity Coach, and Gem Mining make behavior understandable
6. Discovery, incubation, and Atlas feed future edge
7. Graphify and dashboards make the whole thing governable
8. Build Week extended the platform itself, not just one bot

## Slide emphasis

- less emphasis on individual trade performance
- more emphasis on platform architecture and disciplined workflow
- show the judges why this is a Work & Productivity project
